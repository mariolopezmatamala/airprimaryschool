Protection of Personal Data According to LOPDDD

Air Primary School, in compliance with current regulations on the protection of personal data, informs that the personal data collected through the forms on the website: Full Name, Country of Residence, email, age, and other data that the user may voluntarily provide, such as postal address, phone number, are included in specific automated user files.

The automated collection and processing of personal data aim to maintain the commercial relationship and perform tasks of information, education, advice, and other activities specific to Air Primary School.

This data will only be shared with entities that are necessary for the sole purpose of fulfilling the aforementioned purpose.

Air Primary School adopts the necessary measures to ensure the security, integrity, and confidentiality of data in accordance with the provisions of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council, of April 27, 2016, regarding the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and the free movement of such data.

The user may exercise the rights of access, opposition, rectification, and cancellation recognized in the aforementioned Regulation (EU) at any time. These rights can be exercised by the user themselves via email at: staff@airprimaryschool.com.

The user states that all data provided by them are true and accurate and undertakes to keep them updated, communicating any changes to staff@airprimaryschool.com
