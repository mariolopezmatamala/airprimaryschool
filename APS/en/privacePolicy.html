1. Introduction

At APS, we take the privacy of our customers very seriously. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the personal information we gather from you as part of our virtual airline service on IVAO. By using our service, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.

2. What data we process

We collect and process the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Country
  • Email address
  • IVAO VID
  • Discord username
  • Age
  • Other data you may voluntarily provide, such as your address or phone number.

3. How and when we collect personal data

We collect personal information from you when you register to use our virtual airline service on IVAO. We may also collect additional personal information if you wish to apply to become a pilot in our virtual airline.

4. Purposes

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To administer and improve our virtual airline service on IVAO
  • To communicate with you and respond to your inquiries
  • To process your pilot application requests
  • To fulfill our legal obligations.

5. Legal bases for collecting and processing your information

We collect and process your personal information based on our legitimate interest in administering and improving our virtual airline service on IVAO, and fulfilling our legal obligations.

6. APS Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties unless necessary to fulfill our legal obligations or to provide our virtual airline services on IVAO.

7. Disclosure of personal information

We may disclose your personal information in the following circumstances:

  • To fulfill our legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
  • To provide our virtual airline service on IVAO to our service providers and business partners, but only to the extent necessary to provide these services
  • With your prior consent for any other purpose.

8. Security of personal information

We take appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, the transmission of information over the Internet is never completely secure, so we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information transmitted through our service.

9. Access and correction of personal information

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, to rectify any inaccurate personal information, and to delete your personal information. To make a request, please contact us using the contact information provided at the end of this privacy policy.

10. Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes in the way we handle your personal information, we will inform you by posting an updated privacy policy on our website or through other communication channels. We recommend that you review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your personal information.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, you can contact us through the following means:

  • APS Discord
  • Contact
