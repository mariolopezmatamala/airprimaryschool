Certified pilots

We are a virtual airline operating inside IVAO network and our main purpose is to help Peru division training department by attending their students training sessions in order to ensure a minimum required traffic and quality for their virtual ATC rank upgrades.

1. Initial requirements

Anyone who wishes can join us by meeting this minimum requirements criteria.

  • FS3 as a minimum pilot rank.
  • At least 500 flying hours.
  • Be at least 16 years old.

2. Process

If you meet the above criteria, you can continue with the recruiting process:

  • Interview with HR
  • Aeronautical English test
  • Aviation theoretical and practical exam

3. Admission

Once you have passed successfully the entire process mentioned above, you will be able to proudly say you belong to the certified IVAO Peru pilots.
Your initial rank will be as Student until you have participated as a Pilot in at least 3 sessions.

4. Obligations

To remain as a Certified Pilot, you must attend and participate in at least 50% of the monthly sessions conducted, except for a justified reason.
