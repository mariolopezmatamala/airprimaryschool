Certified pilots

About us

The Certified Pilots Group of the Peru Division plays a vital role by providing the Training Department with an adequate number of flights necessary to successfully carry out the exams and practical training of the division. Their contribution translates into the execution of essential maneuvers required to ensure proper validation of the exams.


If you wish to join, go to the registration section. We ask you to carefully read the rules before applying.

Trained Pilots

We form an exceptional team of pilots that stands out for its level of excellence and prestige. We maintain a strong commitment to quality, subjecting our pilots to regular comprehensive assessments and training. This ensures that their skills continue to meet the standards of excellence that our group represents.

Boosting the Division

We play a fundamental role in flying IVAO official exams and practices, directly contributing to the development of the Peruvian Division. We ensure that air traffic controllers acquire the necessary skills and knowledge before taking on their responsibilities in managing air traffic.

e-Learning System

The online learning system is a Moodle-based website where new applicants and pilots will be able to assess their knowledge through small exams and recurrent training assessments to provide the best quality.

FAQ

Is it free?
Our services are and will always be completely free.
Which are the requirements?
The requirements are low and allow the vast majority of users to join the airline:
  • Minimum FS3 Rank
  • 500 hours as a Pilot
  • 16 years of age
Who do I contact to join up?
To access, simply go to the registration section. There, complete each required field and then press the "Send Message" button. Once this is done, the message will be automatically forwarded to the Human Resources department, who will guide you in the next steps to proceed with your admission process. If there's any issue during the process, you can contact Human Resources at rrhh@airprimaryschool.com.
Once I'm in, what do I have to fulfill?
The only obligation pilots have is to attend 50% of the monthly sessions, except for justified reasons.
