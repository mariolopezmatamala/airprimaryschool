Fly with Us

You are expected to have the following minimum knowledge as a requirement:

  • Traffic pattern, entry and exit procedures for control zones, and field crossings.
  • Interception of radials/bearings.
  • Instrumental precision and non-precision approaches, including circling.
  • Effective management of the Z flight plan.
  • Proper handling of holding patterns according to instructions.

If you consider you qualify and want to be part of our team, you must join our Discord Server where we will get in touch with you to continue with the recruiting process
